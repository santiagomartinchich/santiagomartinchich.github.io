Santiago Martinchich

I am an assistant professor (profesor adjunto) at Universidad de la República, Uruguay. My work focuses in dynamical systems, interactions with geometry and topology. I am interested in hyperbolics dynamics, Anosov flows, foliation theory and their interactions with global geometry and topology of manifolds. Most of my work so far has been in partially hyperbolic dynamics.
