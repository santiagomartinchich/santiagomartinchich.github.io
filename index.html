Santiago Martinchich

I am an Assistant Professor (Profesor Adjunto) at Facultad de Economía, Universidad de la República, Uruguay.

I was Postdoc at PEDECIBA (Uruguay) during 2023-2024. I completed my PhD in 2023 at Université Paris-Saclay and Universidad de la República under the supervision of Sylvain Crovisier (Paris-Saclay) and Rafael Potrie (Udelar).

Here you can find my CV.

Research

I work in Dynamical Systems, and interactions with Geometry and Topology.

Particular interests: Partially hyperbolic diffeomorphisms, Anosov flows, foliation theory, manifolds...
